How we deliver stability

FastHotspot uses a Starlink Gen-3 dish as primary backhaul. We pair it with professional routers, QoS rules, local caching and optional 4G backup for unbeatable uptime. Equipment is secured, earthed and professionally mounted.

Key benefits

  • Reliable LEO satellite backhaul (low latency for satellite)
  • Managed hotspot system with vouchers and time limits
  • Local installation and ongoing maintenance in Abuja
  • Power backup options (inverter + battery / solar) to stay online during outages
